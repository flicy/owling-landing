# Project Brief

## One-Liner

We are building an AI companion plush for children and families: not just an English tutor or storytelling toy, but an embodied agent that can talk with a child in real time, remember daily moments, and grow with the child.

## 1-3 Sentence Description

We are building an AI companion plush for children and families: not just an English tutor or storytelling toy, but an embodied agent that can talk with a child in real time, remember daily moments, and grow with the child.

Through voice, memory, and personalized interaction, it captures the child's interests, emotions, learning changes, and family stories, becoming a natural companionship and communication interface between the child, parents, and family.

## Current Focus

This hackathon focuses on "plush companionship: a co-growing embodied agent for children or family scenarios." English learning is the entry point; the real demonstration should be companionship, memory, co-growth, and family communication.

## Core Users

- Child: needs a companion that listens, receives imagination, and grows together.
- Parent: wants a more natural way to understand the child's daily life, emotions, interests, and learning changes.
- Family: needs a gentle relationship mediator so parent-child communication is not only questioning, education, or task management.

## Core Features

- Real-time voice conversation: the child can talk naturally with the plush.
- Proactive companionship: the plush asks what happened today, how the child feels, and whether they want to tell a story.
- Lightweight memory: remembers the child's name, interests, recent events, English learning content, and family members.
- Co-growth: the plush changes through interaction, such as a shared story tree, memory leaves, interest map, or companion level.
- Parent summary: optional daily summaries, interest changes, and parent-child communication suggestions.

## What This Product Is Not

- Not a pure English vocabulary Q&A machine.
- Not a generic storytelling device.
- Not an adult emotional companionship product.
- Not a full online social platform for children.
- Not a child-facing hardware product ready for compliant commercial launch.
